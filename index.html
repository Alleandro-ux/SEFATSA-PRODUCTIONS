About Sefatsa Productions (Pty) Ltd

Sefatsa Productions (Pty) Ltd is a specialised mining-solutions company dedicated to unlocking value from one of the industry's most overlooked assets: high-grade ore lost through historical underground spillage. Across South Africa, decades of production-driven mining have resulted in substantial accumulations of premium-grade material—some dating back to the late 1990s and early 2000s—remaining dormant along underground transport systems. As mines transition from volume-focused output to stable, efficient and cost-disciplined operations, the recovery of this material has emerged as a strategic priority.

Sefatsa Productions delivers that solution through a refined, high-efficiency extraction model. Using lightweight, purpose-designed equipment integrated with superior recovery methodologies, our team is capable of extracting up to 15,000 tons per month over a five-year operational horizon. This approach consistently reduces operating costs by up to 30% while enhancing extraction efficiency by approximately 35%, enabling mines to reclaim value without disrupting core production.

Driven by a highly skilled workforce and a strict commitment to operational excellence, Sefatsa Productions positions itself as a premium optimisation partner—transforming overlooked ore into measurable, sustainable financial gain.
